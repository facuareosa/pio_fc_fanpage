Rooms and common spaces

The Boathouse offers a variety of accommodation options to meet the needs and preferences of its guests. With a total of 5 double rooms, there is a room for every taste. The deluxe room features a private bathtub for relaxing, while the room with extra large bed is perfect for couples seeking comfort and additional space. For those who prefer single beds, we have 2 rooms available.

In addition, the yacht features a spacious living room equipped with comfortable sofas for relaxing. To enjoy the sun, we have sun loungers on the bow. And to enjoy the sea views while having a bite or drink, the terrace features a sofa, tables and chairs.

At The Boathouse, we ensure that our guests have everything necessary for a relaxing and comfortable stay.

Location

We are located in a marina with clothing shops and a wide variety of dining options. Just meters away you will find wide beaches where you can enjoy a day of sun and sea.

From the port, you can also take a boat for a sea ride and see dolphins in their natural habitat. A unique life experience!

Our location is ideal for those seeking a relaxing getaway and yet being close to the action. We are only 20 km from the vibrant center of Málaga and 10 km from the airport, making it easy to arrive and depart.
